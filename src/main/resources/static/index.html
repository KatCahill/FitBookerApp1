About Us

Welcome to FitBookerApp, a cozy and welcoming fitness space founded by two passionate sisters, Jenna and Katie Myers, in 2021. What started as a dream to create a community-driven gym quickly grew into a local hub for fitness lovers of all kinds. Our mission is simple: to provide a personalized fitness experience that makes every member feel seen, supported, and motivated. At FitBookBookeApp, we believe that fitness isn’t just about lifting weights – it’s about lifting spirits, building confidence, and creating a space where everyone feels at home. With a focus on flexibility, accessibility, and community, we offer a range of gym classes designed to fit all levels, from beginners to seasoned athletes. Our goal is to help you discover your inner strength, crush your fitness goals, and, most importantly, have fun along the way! a What drives us every day is the belief that fitness should be for everyone, no matter where they start. We’re here to make your fitness journey enjoyable, challenging, and, above all, empowering. Come join us at FitBookerApp – where fitness meets family, and every step forward is a step toward a stronger, healthier you!
